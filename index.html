LUCA TRAINI

I am a postdoctoral researcher at the Department of Information Engineering, Computer Science and Mathemathics of the University of L'Aquila (Univaq). I received the PhD in Computer Science from Univaq in 2021 under the supervision of Prof. Vittorio Cortellessa.

My research interests broadly lie in software performance engineering; in particular I'm interested in improving software performance assurance in fast-evolving systems, both from a process and a technical perspective.

My current research work includes:

  • ethnografic studies to investigate performance assurance practice and challenges in fast-evolving systems.
  • empirical studies to investigate the impact of maintainance activities (i.e., refactoring) on software performance.
  • development of AI techniques to support debugging of performance issues in (micro)service-based systems.
